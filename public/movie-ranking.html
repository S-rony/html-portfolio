The Best Movies According to Soubhik

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

Drive

"Drive" is a stylish, intense, and atmospheric neo-noir thriller with a mesmerizing performance by Ryan Gosling and a pulsating synth-heavy soundtrack.

First Man

"First Man" is a visually stunning and emotionally gripping portrayal of Neil Armstrong's journey to the moon, anchored by Ryan Gosling's restrained yet powerful performance

Blue Valentine

"Blue Valentine" is a raw, emotionally devastating exploration of love and heartbreak, fueled by powerhouse performances from Ryan Gosling and Michelle Williams.
